# Sable — User Guide

**Sable** is a personal, visual-first app for planning a tattoo journey: collecting
favourite artists, capturing ideas, matching the two together, and keeping an eye on
conventions and studios. It runs in your browser and installs to your phone's home
screen like a native app. Sign in with your account and your data syncs across devices,
with a local copy kept on each device so the app still works offline.

## How the app is organised

The bottom bar mirrors the workflow — see what's next, curate artists, match ideas,
generate concepts. Tap **More (⋯)** for reference pages and admin.

| Bottom bar | | More menu |
|---|---|---|
| **Home** — your shortlist pipeline & what's next | | **Radar** — upcoming conventions |
| **Artists** — collection, ranking & upkeep (Manage) | | **Studios** — where your artists work |
| **Ideas** — tattoo ideas, with Boards as a tab | | **Settings** — backup, account & sign out |
| **AI** — concept generator | | **Help** — a quick guide on your device |

Top-right of every screen: **A+ / A−** changes text size, and **◑ / ◐** switches between
dark and light themes.

The same guidance is built into the app under **More → Help**:

![The in-app Help page](../public/guide/help-overview.png)

> **On a fresh install** your artists, studios and conventions are already loaded.
> Ideas, boards and AI concepts start empty — those are the things you create.

## A typical planning journey

The pieces are designed to feed each other:

1. **Find** an artist on Instagram and **add** them in *Artists → Manage* (handle + name).
2. **Upload** a few of their pieces and **tag** them with styles (e.g. `blackwork`, `fine-line`).
3. **Rank** your collection in *Artists* — drag, nudge, or use swipe-compare — and set
   shortlist statuses. *Home* shows the pipeline: researching → shortlisted → contact next → contacted.
4. **Capture an idea** in *Ideas*, tag it with the same styles, and it instantly **suggests
   matching artists** to link.
5. **Group related ideas** on the *Boards* tab, then **Copy brief** to share a clean summary
   with an artist.
6. Check **More → Radar** for conventions where your shortlisted artists are appearing, and
   **Studios** for where they work and how far that is.
7. **Export a backup** from *More → Settings* so you always hold a restore point.

## Installing on your iPhone

Sable is a Progressive Web App, so it installs without an app store:

1. Open the app's URL in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share** button, then **Add to Home Screen**.
3. Launch it from the new icon — it opens full-screen, in dark mode, like a native app.
