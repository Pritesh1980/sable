Gallery View Concepts

Optimised for widescreen · artist style comparison

Option 01

Filmstrip Rows

Each artist occupies a full-width row. Info pinned left, images scroll horizontally. Scroll down through artists, scroll right through their work.

Strengths
See every artist at a glance. Natural scan pattern. No clicks needed. Best for browsing.
Tradeoffs
Images are smaller. Horizontal scroll per row can feel fiddly on trackpad.
Option 02

Split Pane

Fixed artist sidebar on the left. Click or arrow-key through artists — their full gallery fills the right panel.

Option 03

Comparison Columns

Select 2–3 artists to compare. Each gets a column with independently scrollable images. Like a visual diff.

Option 04

Vertical Lanes

Each artist is a vertical column. Scroll horizontally across artists, vertically within each artist's images. A visual Kanban for art.

Option 05

Table with Expand

Compact list — one row per artist with thumbnail and tags. Click to expand inline into a filmstrip. Minimal when collapsed, rich when expanded.
